Carving at the Casino

Salamanca, NY

Top carvers transform logs to sculptures. And if you have you eye on a piece -- you can take home by winning it in the auction! Carving happens each day in the Event Center parking lot of the Seneca Allegeny Resort & Casino.

Open to the public. Plan a weekend trip with your family or friends. There's no fee to bid in the Saturday and Sunday auctions. Carvings can be paid for with cash, credit card, check, or Player Card Points.
