Auction

There's no fee to bid in the Saturday and Sunday auctions. Carvings can be paid for with cash, credit card, check, or Player Card Points.

Secure your favorite carving before the auction by paying for it at the "Buy It Now" price.

Salamanca Chamber Brew & Wine Fest

Over thirty craft breweries will be attending the Salamanca Chamber's Beer and Wine Fest on Saturday June 4 running from 3 PM - 7PM. Tickets are $15 (presale) or $25 at the door and supports Empire Animal Rescue Society.

More information on the Salamanca Chamber website.

Salamanca, NY

Top carvers transform logs to sculptures. And if you have you eye on a piece -- you can take home by winning it in the auction! Carving happens each day in the Event Center parking lot of the Seneca Allegeny Resort & Casino.

Open to the public. Plan a weekend trip with your family or friends.
